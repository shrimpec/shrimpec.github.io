Do I take a break? YES, OF COURSE!

As people of all ages, young or experienced (respect your elders, kids), we have all gone through trials and tribulations. We have all had our fair share of mental and emotional sturggle.

Depending on how severe, we have reached out or taken matters into our own hands, whether that a distraction or attempts to stay in a positive mindset.

On this very website, you will come across versions of very beloved games, Pong and Flappy Bird. Each of ours differ by a hair, but they are all the same game.

Just like our games, we all have different paths on our journey to good, stable mental states. However, its important that we all do this one very thing...take care of ourselves. It's sometimes necessary to just walk away for a moment and find a distraction. Whether a person to talk to or a game to play (heyy, maybe this website will do just the trick), lets do our best to take care of ourselves.
