I hope that you enjoyed playing these games. Crafting the games had been quite a challenge, but overall, the hardwork paid off. This is much like what happens to us on the daily.

On a regular, we tend to stress ourselves out and don't allow ourselves to take breaks of any kind. Such actions can be found to harm our mental health and not solve emotional issues either. Taking the time to allow our bodies and minds to calm itself allows for stress to not overcome our lives and consume us.

Thus, what do I do to relieve my own stress and anxieties? I turn to distracting myself in any way I can. From treating myself to some coffee and a scone or just playing video games for a few moments out of my day, I find something that helps ease my mind. Whether it takes five minutes or an hour, take the time to care for yourself. PLAY THESE GAMES!! Distract yourself before you face the problems at hand.

Your mental and emotional wellbeing matters. You have to care for them both because when one is off track, the other can go with it. So take care of yourself :)
