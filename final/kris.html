i know, i know. pong and flappy bird were quite the catch. but really, i hope you found them stress relieving!

overworking is so much more normalized than it actually should be. it's important to know that taking breaks is okay and necessary. what we do is enough, and we deserve to rest our bodies and minds without feeling guilty.

did you ask what i do to relieve stress? my personal favorites are working out, drawing, and reading. i'd say playing games is a great choice too and that's really what we're here to help with!

tip: learn the 25 and 5 minute rule. work for 25 minutes and take 5 minute breaks in between so you don't overwhelm yourself. i love doing this when i feel stressed and have trouble managing my time.

lastly, always put yourself first! what matters most is your health and well-being. surprise! it's not school, or work, or anything else. make sure to take care of yourself and your loved ones. balance and consistency is key :)
