Yes, there's more for you! Here are some more well known spots in the city:

The "MET"ropolitan Museum of Art

I know what you may be thinking...boring. BUT, the MET is a signature staple in the Big Apple. At the MET, you can learn about the history of the world through art such as paintings and sculptures in addition to artifacts like musical instruments and tools.

The official MET site!

The High Line

The High Line is an elevated park, greenway and rail trail, measuring a distnace of 1.45 mile long. The High Line was constructed on a former New York Central Railroad on the west side of Manhattan.

Check it out!
Rockefeller Center

Rockefeller Center is a home staple in New York City. All year round, the public area puts up art and decorations in celebration of spirit. With features like food and The Top of The Rock, people keep coming back for more. During the holiday season, Rockefeller Center puts a giant christmas tree for people to come visit. For many New Yorkers, it becomes a tradition to gather with family and take a trip to see the spectacular sight.

Make some plans!
Hudson Yards

Hudson Yards, which used to be an old train yard back in the olden days, is a beautiful plaza full of retail shops. Walking in for the holidays, you are blinded by the beautiful lights in celebration of holiday cheer.

Explore Now!
Central Park

If you live or have ever visited New York and haven't gone to Central Park, you've been living under a rock! In Central Park, you can explore the beautiful outdoors with friends or family, and even by yourself. Central Park has lots of greenery and you can even go and see some turtles swimming in the water. In Central Park, you can particppate in seasonal activities which include ice skating in Wollman's Rink and my favorite, the Central Park Zoo.

The Official Guide!
Bronx Zoo

The Bronx Zoo is a destination of exploration. Take the kids, the friends, or yourself, to explore the beauty of wildlife and nature. From elephants to giraffes, the Bronx Zoo has a variety of animals, great for a earning experience as well as some family time.

Go see some animals!
Nathan's

Want a hot dog? Go to Nathan's. Opened in 1916, Nathan's had been around for years. Along the boardwalk in Coney Island, Nathan's is the BEST place for a hot dog and a great place to grab quality time over some quick fast food.

Madison Square Garden

If you're from a big city, we all have that one stop shop where concerts are just the absolute best. Madison Square Garden, the theater for concerts and live musical entertainment and a place for people to make some baskets. Madison Square Garden is the place to be!
