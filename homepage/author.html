A Little About Me!

My name is Elizabeth!

Unfiltered!!

You can call me Lizzie!

From birth, I grew up in New York City and have been fortunate enough to have parents who allowed me to explore this rich city. I've been able to not only encounter different cultures and backgrounds, but I have been able to emerge myself into such beauties of which come with humanity.

For a teenager, I've had the privilege of not only making memories in some of these places with family, but with friends and even on my own. From exploring Hudson Yards with a friend to seeing the tree at Rockefeller Center every hoilday season with my family, here in New York, there's an opportunity to build relationships and traditions.

"The city that never sleeps" is a place you're going to want to be in life. My New York City is different than yours, but in My New York City, I live my life to the fullest.
