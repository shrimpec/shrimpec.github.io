New York City

The City That NEVER Sleeps!

If you can see it from space, you know it's big. New York is a bright, bold city full of millions of people with dreams of their own. Walk down one block, you can discover a life time of stories.

Before you continue exploring this sight, I would like to provide you with a disclaimer. This is ONLY some of my personal favorites. In New York, there is so much opportunity for adevnture and exploration. So, take a peek into these places and explore something new. I'm doing the same as I walk arond the city, finding some new cracks in the pavement and new specks of paint on the brick walls of buidings. Go find new cracks and crevices, paint sepckles and signatures.

On my site, you will feel as though you are a New Yorker-but don't say that out loud. In New York, don't act like a "tourist."
